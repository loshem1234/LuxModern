About Lux-Modern

Lux-Modern Renovations was started with a simple belief: Dayton homeowners deserve the same care and precision usually reserved for high-end builds, applied to everyday repairs and remodels alike. We treat a leaking faucet with the same seriousness as a full kitchen gut.

We're proud to be part of Dayton's ongoing revival — investing in local crews, local materials where possible, and long-term relationships with the families and neighborhoods we work in across the Miami Valley.
